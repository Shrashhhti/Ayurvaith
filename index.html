The Pink Gold of the Himalayas: A Pure Alternative to Table Salt

Himalayan pink salt is a mineral-rich salt mined from ancient sea beds in the Himalayan foothills. Untouched by modern pollution, its natural pink colour reflects the presence of valuable trace minerals.

How Is Himalayan Pink Salt Better Than Normal Table Salt?

Normal table salt is heavily refined and stripped of minerals, often containing anti-caking agents. Himalayan salt is minimally processed and retains essential minerals like potassium, magnesium, calcium, and iron.

Why Should You Prefer Himalayan Salt?

Himalayan salt offers a cleaner seasoning without artificial additives, a softer and more balanced flavour, and better dietary balance that supports hydration and muscle function.
