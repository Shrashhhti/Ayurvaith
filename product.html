Himalayan Pink Salt

​Himalayan pink salt is a mineral-rich salt mined from ancient sea beds tucked away in the foothills of the Himalayan mountain range. Often called the purest salt on earth, it has remained untouched by modern pollution for millions of years, preserved by volcanic layers. Its distinct rosy hue is a natural indicator of the varied trace minerals it contains.
